Research

Defect Detection Based on Computer Vision | Research Assistant
Mar. 2018 – Present
Advisor: Ruiping Qiao, Associate Professor, XJTU
    Achieved defect detection of electrode coating under industrial production conditions, based on specific method of image binarization.
    Conducted the team to accomplish defect detection of blister drug with method of image segmentation, based on adaptive grayscale processing and special segmentation of HSV color space.
    Solved the loss of accuracy caused by various defects of blister drug, with the help of traditional Convolutional Neural Network.
    Improved the accuracy and application scope of our discriminant program, with the applying of Mask R-CNN.
    The project has passed the mid-term acceptance.

Design of Simple Speech Recognition System | Team Leader
Sep. 2018 – Jan. 2019
Advisor: Yuanlin Zhang, Associate Professor, Institute of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics
    Speech signal is preprocessed by intercepting, framing, windowing and extracting MFCC features.
    Features are compared by using Dynamic Time Warping and appropriate threshold is determined.
    Applied PCA to reduce features dimension and compared effects of different clustering methods such as KNN, Decision Tree and Naive Bayes.
    Visualize features and results of different clustering methods on the 2-D plane with t-SNE.

Research and Application of Few-shot Learning | Research Assistant
Jun. 2018 – Sep. 2018
Advisor: Xueming Qian, Professor, SMILES Lab
    Reproduced the results of a paper about few-shot learning in CVPR 2018, which proposed a method for the neural network to gain the ability of learning to compare.
    Constructed the feature-embedding network and relation network which are used to realize a comparing function and fine-tuned the parameters of two networks to obtain a faster rate of convergence on some simple datasets (such as Omniglot).
    Improved the model to achieve higher accuracy - 3% higher than the original model in average - on more complex datasets (such as mini-Imagenet), with efficient image pre-processing method, appropriate model complexity and innovative structure of input data.
    Retrofitted original model with core idea of LSTM to attain a memory ability for a small number of template images, adapting the model to a large number of categories.
    Replaced the traditional loss function with triplet loss to classify pictures which are outside the category into a new certain category, enhancing its applicability in unknown but real scenarios.
